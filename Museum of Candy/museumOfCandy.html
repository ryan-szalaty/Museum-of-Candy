MUSEUM OF CANDY

Chocolate, caramel, peppermint, jawbreakers, lollipops, and anything else your mind can imagine. Picture walls of licorice, windows of taffy and pillars of joy welcoming you and your family - the one and only Museum of Candy.

PRECIOUS MEMORIES

Nowhere else in the world can you witness 100% natural chocolate being processed in real time while appearing like a river in a tunnel. Indeed, there have also never been certain candy constructions of such fine craftsmanship - only here at the Museum of Candy.

CENTURIES OF HISTORY

Established in the late-1700s, what began as an exhibit of rare candies grew to be the home of the world's most decadent and exquisite sweets, each raised from the hands of passionate chocolatiers and treat enthusiasts.
